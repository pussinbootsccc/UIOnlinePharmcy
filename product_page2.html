    Product Details

    Moisturizes & Soothes Dry, Stuffy Noses.
    • Saline Nasal Mist
    • Non-Medicated
    Contains: An isotonic solution of sodium chloride 0.65% in deionized water, buffered with potassium phosphate / sodium hydroxide to prevent nasal irritation, and the antibacterial and antifungal preservatives disodium EDTA and benzalkonium chloride.

    Ayr helps you breathe better.

    Indications

    Ayr Mist moisturizes nasal passages relieving dry, crusty and inflamed nasal membranes caused by allergies and colds. It is an effective nasal wash for sinuses. Safe and gentle. Doctor recommended.

    Warnings

    Use only as directed.
    Keep out of reach of children.
    The use of this dispenser by more than one person may spread infection.

    Ingredients

    An Isotonic Solution of:
    • Deionized Water
    • Sodium Chloride
    • Sodium Hydroxide Buffer
    • Potassium Phosphate
    • Disodium EDTA
    • Benzalkonium Chloride

    Directions

    Hold upright and squeeze twice in each nostril.
    For drops, tilt head back and apply 2 to 6 drops in each nostril. Use as often as needed.
    Take care not to aspirate nasal contents back into bottle. If spray tip touches nose, rinse with hot water before replacing cap.
